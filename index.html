About Me

He received the M.S. dgree in Control Engineering and the Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi’an, China in 2017 and 2022, respectively. He was a joint Ph.D. Student with the Department of Telecommunications and Information Processing, Ghent University, Ghent, Belgium. He is currently an associate professor with the School of Communications and Information Engineering, Xi’an University of Posts & Telecommunications, Xi’an, China. His research interests include hyperspectral imaging, tensor modeling, and deep learning.
